About Me

Who am i?

I’m an Assistant Professor/Researcher/Blogger/Youtuber

I have a doctorate in English Literature. Currently I am an Assistant Professor, Researcher, Blogger and a Youtuber. The best part of me is still a student. I enrolled for my second (distance education) MA in Women and Gender Studies. GLA University has a wonderful club called the Poetry Club GLAU and I have the honour of being its coordinator. During my bachelor’s and master’s, I had directed and acted in English and Hindi plays. I have also been the commander of 9 Bengal Battalion Women’s contingent NCC. I have had classical training in vocal music and Ravindra sangeet. Last but not the least I have a Junior diploma in Kathak.
